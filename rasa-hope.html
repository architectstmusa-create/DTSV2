Rasa Hope

2025 • Drama • 5.11m 18+

After her sister-in-law's wedding service, a tired mum opts to nap before the reception party, only to awaken and find she’s lost track of more than just the time. Film by Taku Mbudzi (2024) __________ The film title 'Rasa Hope' is a play on Shona and English words: losing sleep, losing Hope (a person) and losing hope. Set within the nuances and complexities of intercultural motherhood in the diaspora, this experimental film, is based on a nightmare I had during the pandemic. I sat on my 3-minute script for four years until I got the unexpected chance to make it in one weekend, for free and for fun, at a time I felt discouraged about my filmmaking career.
