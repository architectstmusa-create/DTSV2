Taxi

2024 • Grit • 11.57m 16+

When a stranger presents Kuda, a taxi driver with a wreckless nature, an offer he can't resist, his life takes a sharp turn. This enticing proposition leads him down a path of darkness, challenging his very sense of right and wrong. "Taxi" is a short film from the mind of Award Winning Director Jonathan Samukange and starring Award Winning Actor Munya Chidzonga, Set against the backdrop of Harare, Zimbabwe.
